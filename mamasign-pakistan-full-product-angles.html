Research-backed simple direction

DIGITAL AUDIENCE
Pakistan mein 116M internet users aur 66.9M social identities hain. Paid ads scale possible hai. 		FREELANCE + IT
IT exports record $3.8B, freelance contribution $708M. Freelancers/software houses strong target hain. 		SME MARKET
SMEs GDP, exports aur employment mein major role rakhti hain. Unka paperwork still messy hai. 		E-COMMERCE
E-commerce policy mein digital payments, seller onboarding, paperless export docs aur MSMEs central hain.

Is liye ad ka first line "signature bhejo" nahi hona chahiye. First line segment ka pain hona chahiye: "proposal approved hai lekin payment delay hai", "offer letter pending hai", "client invoice aur agreement scattered hain", "forms WhatsApp par gum ho jate hain."

15 full-product paid ad angles
Har angle mein sirf e-sign nahi, relevant feature bundle pitch karo.
01HR teams aur recruiters
Offer letter se onboarding tak

Feature bundle: templates, offer letters, bulk send, reminders, identity checks, signed certificates, folders.
Pain: Candidate select hota hai lekin offer letter sign karne mein delay, HR bar bar follow-up karti hai.
Angle: "Candidate ko same day offer bhejo, sign karwao, aur onboarding record ek jagah rakho."
Ad hook: "Offer letter pending hai? Hiring slow mat karo."
Creative: HR dashboard, mobile signing, automatic reminder, completed certificate.
Landing headline: HR documents ko print-scan ke baghair complete karo.

Best launch sequence

Phase 1: Freelancers, agencies, HR, software houses, SMEs. In mein pain strong hai aur ad creative easy hai.
Phase 2: real estate, schools, accountants, training institutes, consultants.
Phase 3: e-commerce sellers, construction/interior, event vendors, developers/API users.

Har ad mein 3 cheezen dikhao: problem, MamaSign workflow, result. Product features ko bundle mein dikhana hai, long list mein nahi.
Ad messaging rule

Wrong: "Online signature app use karein."
Right: "Proposal, agreement aur invoice ek branded link se bhejo. Client mobile se approve/sign kare, aap dashboard se status track karo."

Pakistan mein "single feature" pitch se zyada "business workflow" pitch sell karega.
