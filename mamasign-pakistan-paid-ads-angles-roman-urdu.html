PAKISTAN MARKET STRATEGY

MamaSign ke liye 15 paid ads angles jo Pakistan mein sell kar sakte hain.

Is report ka maqsad simple hai: har ad angle ko easy words mein explain karna taake creative team, media buyer, aur sales team same message par kaam karein. Pakistan mein log feature nahi khareedtay, woh apna daily masla solve karna chahte hain: printing, scanning, WhatsApp approvals, HR documents, property paperwork, client follow-up, aur trust.
Simple market logic
Pakistan mein MamaSign ko "e-signature software" ke naam se sell karna weak hoga. Behtar angle yeh hai: "document bhejo, client mobile se sign kare, aur signed PDF record ke sath mil jaye." Logon ko pehle yeh samjhana hoga ke unka paper wala kaam ab phone se ho sakta hai.

Audience
SME owners, HR, agencies, freelancers, real estate, schools, clinics. 		Main pain
Print, scan, courier, follow-up, missing approvals, aur informal WhatsApp deals. 		Best promise
Phone se sign, PDF record, audit trail, aur client ko account banane ki zaroorat nahi.
Budget ka simple plan
Pehle 30 din mein sab se pehle Tier A angles test karo. 60% budget Google Search + retargeting par rakho, kyun ke yahan intent zyada hoti hai. 40% budget Meta/TikTok short demo videos par rakho, kyun ke Pakistan mein awareness aur education video se fast hoti hai.
15 easy-word angles

01No printing. No scanning. No chasing.
SME owners aur office admins ke liye		Tier A
Easy explanation: Pakistan mein choti companies abhi bhi contract print karti hain, sign karwati hain, phir scan karti hain. Yeh angle unko seedha yeh batata hai ke yeh sab kaam phone se ho sakta hai.

Ad hook: "Ab contract print karna band karo. PDF bhejo, client phone se sign kare."
Creative: Ek side printer/scanner, doosri side mobile par signed PDF.
CTA: Apna pehla document online sign karwao.

Final recommendation

Important legal note
Ads mein yeh claim na karo ke MamaSign har legal document ka replacement hai. Safe wording yeh hai: "common business documents ke liye online signature workflow". Property transfer, wills, negotiable instruments, ya government-certified digital signature claims legal review ke baghair use na karo.
